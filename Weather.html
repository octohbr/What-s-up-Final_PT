Weather Skies

The sky changes not just with the time of day but also with the weather. Sometimes it's clear and bright, sometimes full of clouds, and sometimes stormy or rainy. Here we'll see how the sky looks in different weather and what makes each scene unique.

Sky in Every Weather

The Sunny Sky

A sunny sky is clear and bright, with warm sunlight spreading across everything below. It makes the day feel cheerful and full of energy, perfect for outdoor adventures or just enjoying the warmth.

The Cloudy Sky

Cloudy skies are gray or white, softening the sunlight and creating a calm or moody atmosphere. The clouds can move slowly or quickly, and sometimes they hint that rain might be coming.

The Rainy Sky

Rainy skies are heavy with dark clouds and often let streaks of rain fall to the earth. The air smells fresh, and everything feels cool and quiet as the drops tap the ground.

The Stormy Sky

Stormy skies are dramatic, with dark clouds, flashes of lightning, and strong winds. They feel powerful and intense, reminding us how strong nature can be.

The Foggy Sky

As the sun goes down, the blues of the sky grow darker and deeper. The shift feels calm and peaceful, with the first stars slowly peeking out. This transition prepares the sky for night, carrying a sense of quiet beauty that feels both relaxing and mysterious.

“Take a moment to notice what kind of sky you have right now—sunny, cloudy, or stormy?”
