What's up?

A little blog about the skies above — clouds, sunsets, stars, and everything flying through them.

The Clouds

Clouds are the soft shapes we see in the sky every day, making the world feel calm and dreamy, and they come in all sorts of shapes for you to imagine!

The Sky

The sky is the big, beautiful canvas above us, changing colors from sunrise to sunset, with clouds, sun, and stars that make every moment feel special!

The Universe

The universe is everything beyond Earth—the big space with stars, planets, and galaxies. From the shining stars to our own Milky Way, there’s always something cool to see when we look up.

The Birds

Birds are the lively friends of the sky we see every day, singing, flying, or resting, and they come in all sizes and colors to brighten our world

The Weather Skies

Weather is the sky's way of changing moods—rainy, sunny, or cloudy—making every day feel different and interesting

Others

Other sky wonders are the surprising things we see—like planes, rainbows, shooting stars, or glowing clouds—that make looking up fun!

What's up?

In a world that often feels loud and rushed, these simple wonders—clouds drifting above, birds in flight, the glow of a sunrise, the silence of the night sky—remind us to pause. They are small pieces of beauty we can easily overlook, yet they ground us, inspire us, and connect us to something bigger than ourselves. This space is a reminder to slow down, look up, and appreciate the quiet magic that surrounds us every day.

“Up there is the biggest screen of all—the sky. It's with us every single day, yet we miss so much of it because we're too busy looking down. Maybe it's time to glance up more often.”
